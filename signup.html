Become a Judge

In order to become a certified OJA Judge you must:

  • Fill out this form
  • Agree to the Constitution and Code of Conduct
  • Pay the $25 member dues
  • Pass your R1 and P1 Exams
  • Receive an EM1 certification

    • More information on the certification process can be found here: Level Definition Document
